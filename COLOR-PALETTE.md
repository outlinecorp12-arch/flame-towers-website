## 🎨 Brand Psychology Summary

**Overall Effect**: Premium, Modern, Trustworthy

- **Navy** establishes luxury and professionalism
- **Gold** adds warmth and exclusivity
- **Turquoise** introduces energy and innovation
- **Creams** provide breathing room and elegance
- **Earth tones** (Copper, Burgundy) add sophistication

**Cultural Fit**: Perfect for Azerbaijan's market, balancing international luxury standards with local aesthetic preferences.
